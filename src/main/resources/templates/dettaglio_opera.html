Vincent van Gogh (1853 - 1890), Paris, December 1887-February 1888 (qui ci va anno)

oil on canvas, 65.1 cm x 50 cm( qui va tecnica)


Van Gogh presented himself in this self-portrait as a painter, holding a palette and paintbrushes behind his easel. He showed that he was a modern artist by using a new painting style, with bright, almost unblended colours.
